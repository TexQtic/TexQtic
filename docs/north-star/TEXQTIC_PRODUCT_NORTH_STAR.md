# TEXQTIC — PRODUCT NORTH STAR V2
## (Balanced Authority Model)

**Status:** 🔒 Locked & Economically Aligned

**Purpose:** Define TexQtic as compliance-governed infrastructure that enables capital flow without becoming a financial intermediary.

---

## 1️⃣ Core Definition

TexQtic is a **compliance-first, trade-chain-native operating system** for regulated textile trade.

**Dominant Axis: Regulatory Defensibility.**

TexQtic enforces compliance state across the supply chain:

**Yarn → Fabric → Processing → Garment → Buyer.**

**Trade can proceed only when compliance gates are satisfied.**

---

## 2️⃣ Governed Liquidity Doctrine

TexQtic enables liquidity **only when compliance state permits it**.

Liquidity is:

- **Triggered** by verified compliance
- **Executed** by licensed financial partners
- **Conditioned** by risk intelligence
- **Structured** outside TexQtic's custody

**TexQtic does not:**

- Hold funds
- Lend capital
- Underwrite credit
- Guarantee transactions

**TexQtic provides:**

- Compliance validation
- Risk scoring
- Settlement trigger authorization
- Event immutability

**Capital follows compliance.**

---

## 3️⃣ Strategic Moat

TexQtic creates a **Network of Necessity** by:

- Making compliance non-bypassable
- Becoming required for DPP validation
- Conditioning financial flows on verified state
- Owning cross-system regulatory orchestration

**Liquidity strengthens governance.**
**Governance controls liquidity.**

---

## 4️⃣ Growth Logic

We do not optimize for volume.

**We optimize for:**

- Regulatory pressure
- Risk exposure
- Financial certainty

**Growth follows compliance enforcement.**

---

## 5️⃣ Risk Containment

Financial execution handled by:

- Licensed PSPs
- Regulated escrow custodians
- Bank financing partners

**TexQtic remains infrastructure.**

**That keeps doctrine intact.**

---

## PART IV — 3-Year Monetization Roadmap

### YEAR 1 — Verification Revenue Layer

**Goal:** Revenue without regulatory exposure.

**Products:**

- Real-time certification verification API
- DPP readiness audits
- Compliance dashboards
- Regulatory exposure scoring

**Revenue Model:**

- SaaS subscriptions (brands)
- Per-verification API fees
- Annual regulatory compliance contracts

**Target Customers:**

- Brands selling into EU/US
- Large manufacturers with export exposure

**Narrative:**
Reduce regulatory liability risk.

---

### YEAR 2 — Compliance-Gated Settlement Layer

**Goal:** Introduce transaction revenue safely.

**Add:**

- Compliance-triggered escrow release logic
- Settlement validation events
- Transaction verification fees

**Financial structure:**

- Partner with licensed PSP
- TexQtic charges infrastructure fee (0.2–0.5%)
- No custody. No underwriting.

**Revenue expands from SaaS → SaaS + Transaction.**

---

### YEAR 3 — Capital Efficiency Layer

**Goal:** Unlock liquidity without becoming lender.

**Add:**

- Reputation-based risk scoring API
- Financing marketplace (bank partners)
- Early payment access for compliant suppliers

**TexQtic revenue:**

- Referral fees
- Risk intelligence subscription
- Capital acceleration analytics

**Banks provide capital.**
**TexQtic provides risk insight.**

1. You never custody funds

Liquidity increases moat.
