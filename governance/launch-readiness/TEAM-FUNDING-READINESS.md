## 1. Purpose

This document tracks team capability gaps, contractor/co-founder needs, external support
requirements, and incubator/seed readiness criteria.

TexQtic is currently a founder-led solo build. This document answers:
1. What team gaps could block or slow launch and pilot?
2. What does TexQtic need before it can operate at pilot scale?
3. What does TexQtic need to qualify for incubator or early funding?
4. What decisions need to be made about when to hire vs. contract?
